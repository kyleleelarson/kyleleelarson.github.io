Kyle Larson

Rényi Institute of Mathematics, Budapest

Background

I am a postdoc at the Rényi Institute of Mathematics in Budapest, Hungary. Previously I was a postdoc at Michigan State University during the academic year 2016-2017. I obtained my PhD from the University of Texas at Austin in December 2015.

Research

My research is in low-dimensional topology. In particular, I am interested in the theory of smooth 4-manifolds and its connections to knot theory and 3-manifolds.

You can download my papers on the arXiv, or download my CV.
